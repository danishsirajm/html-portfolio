My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Oppenheimer

In the course of World War II, physicist J. Robert Oppenheimer is
selected by Lt. Gen. Leslie Groves Jr. to contribute to the classified
Manhattan Project. Over several years, Oppenheimer and his team of scientists
dedicate themselves to the development and design of the atomic bomb. The
culmination of their efforts occurs on July 16, 1945, when they witness
the inaugural nuclear explosion, marking a profound and irreversible shift in
the course of history.

Naruto

Naruto, penned and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto, is a Japanese manga series that
narrates the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a youthful ninja aspiring for acknowledgment
among his peers and harboring dreams of attaining the esteemed position of Hokage, the
leader of his village.

Naruto: Shippuden

Naruto Uzumaki is a boisterous and energetic teenage ninja on a quest for validation
and acknowledgment as he strives to attain the esteemed position of Hokage in his village.
